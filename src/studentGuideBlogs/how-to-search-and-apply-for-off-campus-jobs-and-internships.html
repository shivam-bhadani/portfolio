Referral:

  • Before applying to any job opening, take a referral from any company employee by sending him/her your resume and job id/link.
  • Once you get the referral, you'll receive a unique referral link in your email for that job. Apply through that link.

Cold Email:

To HR/EM/CEO/CTO.

Do hit and trial to the below emails:

  • <firstName>.<lastName>@<company_email>
  • <firstName><lastName>@<company_email>
  • <firstName>_<lastName>@<company_email>
  • <firstName>@<company_email>

<company_email> is generally company's website like for uber - uber.com

Hackathon and Coding Contest

Participate in hiring challenges such as hackathons and coding contests. They have a high probability of responding back.

Below are some websites that conduct these challenges:

  • Unstop: https://unstop.com
  • Hackerearth: https://hackerearth.com/jobs/
